Hello! My name is Veronica Lin and I am currently a rising senior attending UC San Diego. My passion is to explore new fields and go beyond my boundaries - to develop quality products that will positively impact and easen the lives of others. To me, coding is an art to be refined. It's capabilities are limitless.

I started as a Computer Science tutor at UCSD, teaching students Java concepts. Then, I started working with the startup Ixir Design as a Full-Stack Developer and worked on building a restaurant menu application that allowed users to send customized restaurant orders from their phones to the kitchen using NFC technology. Finally, I began interning at Mitchell International and Boeing. I have worked on a series of different types of softwares in both front-end and back-end. I've helped improve usability challenges, enhanced softwares, and increased the stability of API Endpoints.

I am eager to learn and strongly value only putting out my best work. I have yet more to explore.

My journey has just begun.
